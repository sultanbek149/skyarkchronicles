SKYARK MULTIVERSESkyArk マルチバース

House Of Heroes

Once upon a time, Satoshi Nakamoto was born into a bleak world of nothingness. A God of Creation, Satoshi created the SkyArkVerse, a safe world for all intelligent life. But where there is light, darkness will exist. Monsters rose from the deep, devastating the SkyLands and horrified by the invasion, Satoshi must choose the heroes whom he will fight with, in order to return peace back to the SkyArkVerse.

Legends Arise

The SkyArkVerse thrived after Satoshi Nakamoto’s sacrifice. However, the hard won peace is about to shatter. A gatekeeper is murdered and a bridge between two worlds has been left open. The Skylegends must battle the hellish creatures once more in order to safeguard all of the SkyArkVerse!

RE: Skyarkverse

RE: SkyArkVerse is the rebirth of SkyArkVerse, where the new parallel worlds are bridged and also created. In this SocialFi Open World, SkyHeroes and SkyLegends must rebuild their world from the destruction of war. Players will be able to explore a new world, socialize with friends, and build their own lands!

SKYTOKENSスカイトークン

SKYTOKENS

SkyTokens are divided into two types of tokens: SkyArk Chronicles (SAC) as the primary governance token and SkyArk Universe platform utility token.

SAC tokens powers the entire SkyArk Chronicles ecosystem. The tokens are gathered in games to grow your NFTs. Play-and-Own within the SkyArk Universe.

FEATURES機能

PLAY-TO-OWN

SkyArk Chronicles offers a Play-to-Own concept, a free-to-play blockchain game that allows players to own or trade in-game assets. SkyArk will focus on developing a fun gaming experience for all to enjoy.

INTEROPERABLE NFT

SkyArk Chronicles' NFTs are interoperable across the different titles in different forms and gameplay. In other words, you will have the capability to exchange and make use of your in-game assets throughout the multiple game titles of SkyArk Chronicles.

SKYENGINE

SkyEngine is SkyArk's proprietary NFT Engine, provided as the bridge between traditional brands and the Web3.0 metaverse. SkyArk aims to build a sustainable crypto gaming ecosystem using SkyEngine solution by converting conventional games into high quality blockchain games.

GAMEFI-AS-A-SERVICE

SkyArk aims to revolutionize the GameFi experience and build a multiverse of IPs that facilitates co-creation, play and growth in the metaverse. With our SkyEngine solution, SkyArk offers GameFi as a Service (GAAS) to game studios who are looking to shift to Web3.0 and enter the GameFi world.

SKYARK MULTIVERSE

Discover life in the SkyArk Multiverse through different experiences. Whether it is a fantasy or modern urban setting, immerse yourself in different worlds, stories and characters. Progress through a variety of jobs, rare equipment or socialize with friends. Build and personalize your own lands!
